Our Company

Employment opportunities for Professionals

Hibernia-Sino is a travel insurance company who specializes in travel between Ireland and China. The luggage insurance of Hibernia-Sino can provide customers with the safest luggage protection, which is not only reflected in the protection of luggage during the trip, but also reflected in the later claim processing.

In case of luggage damage or loss, the customer service staff will receive the customer's claim feedback in the first time, and timely and effective communicate with the customer to negotiate the claim scheme. The company will give you the most thoughtful service, so that you can relax to enjoy the comfort and pleasure of travel.

We specialize in Irish & Chinese travel insurance, to give you the perfect travel security services.
