Baggage Delay and Loss Loss of Checked Baggage During the period covered by this insurance, if the insured's checked baggage under the control of the transport organization loses or accidentally damages due to the liability of the transport organization or any other third party while traveling abroad, the insurer will be liable for compensation in accordance with the stipulations of this insurance, in accordance with the actual value of the baggage or the lower cost of repair. The allowance for each accident is 20% or $50, whichever is higher.

(The maximum amount of compensation for sports equipment and accessories is 25% of the total insurance amount.)
Loss of carry-on luggage During the insurance period, if the insured's carry-on baggage is lost or accidentally damaged due to the following reasons during his overseas travel, the insurer shall be liable for compensation in accordance with the provisions of this additional insurance, in accordance with the actual value of the baggage or the lower cost of repair, up to the amount insured under this additional insurance specified in the insurance policy. (1) Theft or robbery; (2) Traffic accidents; (3) Fires, explosions, storms, lightning strikes, floods, avalanches and earthquakes; (4) Acts of third parties.

(20% or $50 yuan for each accident, whichever is higher; 50% for photography, photography, video equipment or related accessories; 25% for sports equipment and accessories; and 10% for gifts and souvenirs.)
Delays in Checking Luggage Where the insured checks baggage for overseas travel during the insurance period, the insurer shall indemnify the insured according to the amount of insurance specified in the insurance policy if the checked baggage under the control of the transport agency has not arrived at the intended destination within the time specified in the insurance policy after the public transport vehicle it is traveling on has arrived at the intended destination.

(every 6 hours delay compensation $100, 6 hours start compensation, the maximum amount of compensation is the amount of insurance)
